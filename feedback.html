Infinity Affairs

ToyStory Buzz Lightyear bullshit

across this extraterrestrial universe!

I have seen so much in my time

on this celestial soil, still the waters

flood the cities of space-time continuums,

Biblical Noah rescues some three-eyed,

four-winged creature from the wrath

of our merciless God. Cannonball crush

on a war-torn country, Hershey kiss

for corporate love affairs that human

beings (and Human Resources) will call

citation, censored *#%^#%@&

for the respect of the privacy and public

damnation welcomes the complicit. Moon

-y eyed boys practice violence on foreign

objects contrived from cobalt ruins

of conspiracy theorists’ UFO destinies,

while the children are begging to be

believed. He razes villages before Empires,

scatters Babel-tongued bodies across borders

before they are biopsied. Spoon politicians

into brine and they will emerge wrinklier

and with more bite. Save the blood,

they almost resemble me and you.

The Kuiper Belt wears down the loops

of Neptune’s orbit, and the Sea

rejoices when the icy, frosted donut dissolves

in volumes that burn

through Venice and somewhere south

of the great Caviar-Corn Crevasse,

where Helios rests from that treacherous

8-minute journey and the dude before Galileo

rolls in his grave, along with the other fuckers

affluent with despair,

on God’s Grand Earth.
